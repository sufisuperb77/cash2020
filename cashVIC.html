ABOUT CASHVIC2020

"Expanding the Possibilites"
CASH Virtual Innovation Competition 2020 (CASHVIC2020) is organized by DSR, CGV2, CASD, Universiti Putra Malaysia. Featuring innovations from diverse fields of health, education and sustainable development, the objectives of the event are:


  • To share knowledge and provide an international discussion and exhibition for students, academics, scientific researchers for exchanging new ideas as well as latest developments in the area of health, education and sustainable development.

  • Provides a virtual platform for educators, students, and the public to participate and showcase their innovations.
